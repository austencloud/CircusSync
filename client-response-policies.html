Special Phrases & Situations

Cultural Themes

"While we specialize in high-energy circus-style performances, we want to clarify that we do not have performers specifically trained in [specific cultural dance/performance style]. However, we are experienced in adapting our costumes, energy, and music selection to complement your theme and deliver a visually stunning and appropriate show."

Fire Permits

"Regarding fire permits: requirements vary by location and venue type. Often, the venue coordinator or local fire department can clarify if a permit is needed. If permitting is required, we can connect you with industry professionals who specialize in navigating this process, though they do charge an additional fee for their services. We are fully insured and prioritize safety above all else."

Holiday Premium

"Please note that since your event falls on [holiday], the quoted price includes a holiday premium to compensate our performers for working on this special day."

Space Limitations

"Thank you for providing the dimensions. A [X]x[X] performance space is a bit tight for some of our largest fire props like large fire wings. However, we can absolutely tailor the performance, focusing on props like poi, staff, fans, and fire eating, to fit safely within that area while still delivering an impressive and high-impact show."

Parade Clarification (Balloons)

"For parades, walking and twisting complex balloons simultaneously can be challenging and slow. Would you prefer the balloon twister to walk in the parade making simpler, quicker designs, or would it be better for us to be stationed in a designated spot along the parade route to create more elaborate balloons for attendees as they pass by?"
